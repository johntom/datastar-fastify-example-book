Loss Control

Bergen Risk Managers believes the proper risk management of any insured or self-insured entity necessitates the cost-effective identification, reduction, and occasionally elimination of first and third-party liability exposures. To that end, we offer safety and loss control services designed to reduce exposure and, ultimately, future claim costs.

While services will need to be tailored to the individual insured or self-insured account, some of the available services are:
  • Back/Lifting injury exposure and training
  • Driver Safety seminars
  • MVR Review and identification of problem drivers
  • Work Safety exposures identification for employees
  • Site Inspections to identify exposures and recommend solutions
  • Control alternatives recommendations
  • Cost evaluation of control alternatives
  • Periodic Loss History Review for frequency/severity problems
  • Cost/Benefit Analysis of loss control options
  • OSHA compliance review
  • ADA compliance review
  • Employee drug screening
  • Environmental Testing
  • Life Safety Inspections
  • Employee Stress testing
